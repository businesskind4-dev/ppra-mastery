Our Mission

PPRA Mastery exists to empower procurement professionals across Botswana with accessible, rigorous digital tools for mastering the Public Procurement Act. We believe that a well-trained procurement workforce is the foundation of transparent, efficient, and ethical public spending.

This platform transforms the legal text of the Act into an interactive learning experience — combining study, self-assessment, and professional certification in one place.

About the Creator

Katlo Galenamong

Botswana's procurement landscape is evolving rapidly — and eProcurement is not just an option, it is the future. But technology alone is not enough. True progress comes from professionals who understand the theory behind the systems they operate. We cannot improve what we do not understand. My mission is to build that foundational knowledge across our industry, so that Botswana's procurement professionals are not merely trained to follow procedures, but educated to lead transformation.
